Workflow

1. First, think through the problem. Read the codebase and write a plan in the tasks/todo.md.
2. The plan should be a checklist of todo items.
3. Check in with me before starting work. I'll verify tha plan.
4. Then, complete the todos one by one, marking them off as you go.
5. At every step, give me a high-level explanation of what you changed.
6. Kepp every change simple and minimal. Avoid big rewrites.
7. Go through the code you just wrote and confirm it follows all security best practices. Check that no sensitive data is left in the frontend, and that there are no vulnerabilities an attacker could exploit.
8. At the end, add a review section in todo.md summarizing the changes.
